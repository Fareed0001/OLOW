Our Goals

Our goal is to empower people with disabilities in Africa and ensure an inclusive system that gives them equal and accessible opportunities.
We do this by:

Advocacy

Speaking up for the rights of people with disabilities and working to change policies and attitudes that may be discriminatory or limiting.

Education

Creating awareness by educating people about the challenges about challenges people with disability face and uplifting them by providing them with skills, knowledge and resources needed to become independent.

Accessability and Support

Creating a community where people with disabilities can connect, and ensuring physical spaces and technologies accessible are accessible to PWD
