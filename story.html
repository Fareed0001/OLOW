Our Story

One Life One World is a registeredtransactional non-profit organisation in France and Nigeria. Our goal is to give back home by empowering PWD and advocating inclusive accessibility to essential needs and help Africans in Europe integrate into the system.

One Life One World is a regigistered non-profit organization in France and Nigeria established in 2022. It was born from out experiences as Africans who migrated Europe for greener pastures. We noticed how people with disabilities were included and respected in the system. There were dedicated spaces reserved for PWD on the bus. I hardly ever seelike that back in Nigeria. Ever since, our goal has been to ensure an inclusive system that gives people with disabilities equal and accessible opportunities in Africa.
